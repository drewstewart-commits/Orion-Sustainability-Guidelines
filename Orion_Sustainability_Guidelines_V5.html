Sustainability Guidelines
for Onsite Activations

All onsite partners must comply with the guidelines outlined in this document.

Thank you for choosing to host or participate in an event at The Orion Amphitheater. The Orion is proud to be a leader in sustainability in North Alabama, and environmental stewardship is a core operational pillar of our venue.

Our commitment to reducing waste includes a venue-wide ban on single-use plastics and Styrofoam, a robust recycling program, and the use of evergreen marketing and promotional materials. Since opening in 2022, these initiatives have resulted in the following achievements:

Compliance & Violations

Failure to comply may result in fines, cleanup fees, or removal from Orion property.

  • Improper waste disposal, littering, or violations of the single-use plastic ban $100 fine per offense
  • Oil spills Cost of cleanup plus an additional 10% penalty
  • Strike system Each violation results in a strike on the partner's account. One warning is permitted. Two strikes may result in suspension from Orion property and events for a period determined by Orion Amphitheater Management.
Single-Use Plastic & Styrofoam Ban

Single-use plastic or Styrofoam items are not permitted anywhere on the Orion campus.

  • Compostable plastics (e.g., PLA) are included in this ban. Do not purchase a product because it says "Compostable" — there are many plastic pretenders.
  • Any plastic product intended to be discarded after one use is considered single-use, regardless of design or labeling.
Category Guidelines
Water Bottles
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use plastic water bottles are not permitted within the Orion campus. The ban also applies to donated water bottles. ✓ Alternatives Guests are encouraged to bring their own reusable water bottles to refill at the filtered water refill stations. If packaged water is required, The Orion has approved solutions available. Place purchase orders with the sustainability coordinator 14 days in advance.
Packaged Beverages
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use plastic beverage bottles are not permitted.
  • Soda, juice, electrolyte beverages and tea in single-serving plastic bottles
 ✓ Alternatives Beverages served in cans, cardboard, or from bulk dispensers.
Serveware
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use plastic or styrofoam serveware is not permitted. Compostable plastics (PLA) are included in the single-use plastic ban. Plastic products thrown away after one use are considered single-use regardless of design.
  • Plastic cups and ramekins
  • Compostable plastic cups
  • Styrofoam plates
  • Plastic forks, spoons, and knives
  • Plastic straws
  • Plastic platters
 ✓ Alternatives Reusable serveware should be used whenever possible. The event coordinator will assist with rental options. Glass containers are only permitted on a case-by-case basis.

If single-use serveware is required, The Orion has approved compostable products. Place purchase orders 14 days in advance.

Condiments
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Individual condiments packaged in plastic.
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise packets
  • Sugar packets
  • Individual coffee creamers
  • Coffee pods
  • Sauce cups
 ✓ Alternatives Condiments served in refillable containers.
  • Insulated creamer carafes
  • Condiment pumps
  • Salt and pepper shakers
  • Sugar shakers/pour dispensers
  • Glass bottles, squeeze bottles, or pump dispensers
  • Dredge shakers for seasonings
Generators
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Restricted Generators should only be used when shore power connections are unavailable, not as a primary option. Usage must comply with local fire code. The Safety, Health, and Security Manager may deny and revoke generator usage upon inspection. ✓ Alternatives Vendors needing electrical connections should request areas where shore power is available or where extension cords may be used.
Snacks
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-serving snacks packaged in plastic.
  • Individual chip bags
  • Plastic candy wrappers
  • Baked goods wrapped in plastic
 ✓ Alternatives Unpackaged snacks purchased in bulk or provided by a caterer.
Advertisements
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use, take-away flyers cannot be distributed on the Orion campus. ✓ Alternatives Display boards, posters, banners, or other informational materials that limit printing are encouraged.
Decorations
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Balloons, glitter, and confetti are not permitted within the Orion campus. ✓ Alternatives Reusable or easily recyclable decorations.
  • Flags & cloth bunting
  • Reusable banners
  • Decorative paper arches
  • Paper lanterns
Promotional Items & Gifts
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use plastic items or items that will quickly become trash. All promotional items and gifts must be approved by the sustainability coordinator at least 14 days in advance. ✓ Alternatives
  • Food items
  • Reusable tote bags & water bottles
  • Branded apparel & posters
  • Koozies, coasters, magnets, buttons, pins
  • Stainless steel souvenir cups
  • Reusable utensil packs
Swag Bags
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Pre-made, single-use plastic "swag bags" are not permitted. ✓ Alternatives Promotional items should be prominently displayed, allowing guests to choose which items they would like to take.
Shopping & Gift Bags
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Single-use plastic bags are not permitted for distribution. ✓ Alternatives Reusable bags or paper bags with recycled content. Orion branded reusable tote bags may be ordered upon request.
Waste
Not PermittedSuggested Alternatives
✕ Prohibited Aluminum, cardboard, and glass waste generated on site cannot be thrown in the garbage. ✓ Provided Recycling bins, signage, and general recycling assistance is provided free of charge.
